Audience Creator

By discovering and, analyzing and comparing subsets of your customer databases, you will be able to formulate more effective ways for communicating with and engaging them. Use the customer explorer to discover interesting and valuable homoeous customer and visitor groups, compare groups to understand the differences between the members of each group, and reveal how members migrate from on group to another over time.
